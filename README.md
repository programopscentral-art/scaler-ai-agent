> AI-powered pre-call briefs and personalised PDFs for Scaler BDAs вЂ” generated by Gemini 2.5 Flash, delivered on WhatsApp, gated behind a human approval step.

---

## What I Built

A full-stack web app where an evaluator enters BDA and evaluator WhatsApp numbers, inputs a lead's profile and call transcript (or uploads an audio recording), and the system: (1) sends the BDA a scannable pre-call brief on WhatsApp вЂ” persona analysis, resonant angles, likely objections, and a suggested opening line they can literally say on the call; (2) generates a 2вЂ“3 page personalised PDF that addresses each of the lead's specific questions from the call with concrete reasoning tied to their profile; (3) holds the PDF behind a BDA approval gate вЂ” approve, edit, or skip вЂ” before anything reaches the lead.

## One Failure I Found

When Meera's transcript is short (under 100 words), Gemini extracts only the surface questions and misses the emotional subtext вЂ” the underlying fear of disappointing her parents if she turns down a government job. The PDF becomes technically correct but emotionally flat and unlikely to move her. Fix: a second Gemini pass targeting underlying fears when transcript length is below threshold.

## Scale Plan

At 100,000 leads/month the first bottleneck is PDF generation latency вЂ” reportlab plus two sequential Gemini calls takes 8вЂ“12 seconds per lead, and a single uvicorn worker queues up immediately. Fix: background job queue (Celery + Redis) so PDF generation runs async after the call ends. Second constraint: Gemini Flash's free-tier rate limit (1,500 RPM) hits at roughly 25 concurrent users вЂ” paid tier or exponential backoff batching solves it.

## AI Guardrails (enforced in every prompt)

1. **No fabricated statistics** вЂ” Use "based on Scaler's published outcomes", never invented percentages.
2. **No fabricated salaries** вЂ” Use reasoning ("a 40вЂ“60% jump is typical for this profile"), not invented figures.
3. **Acknowledge uncertainty** вЂ” "based on publicly available information" when unsure.
4. **Use the lead's exact words** вЂ” Every PDF section paraphrases or quotes what they actually said.
5. **Categorise every question** вЂ” `roi/cost`, `curriculum_depth`, `career_outcome`, `program_fit`, `personal_doubt`.

## Known Limitations

- **Short transcripts** вЂ” Under ~100 words, Gemini extracts surface-level questions and misses emotional subtext. Fix: second Gemini pass targeting underlying fears.
- **Twilio 1600-char limit** вЂ” Handled via auto-chunking at paragraph boundaries (`whatsapp_service.py`). Prompt instructs Gemini to stay under 1400 chars.
- **PDF generation latency** вЂ” reportlab + two Gemini calls takes 8вЂ“12 s per lead. At scale, move to a background job queue (Celery + Redis).
- **Sandbox only** вЂ” Twilio WhatsApp Sandbox requires each recipient to send a join code. Production use requires a Twilio approved WhatsApp Business sender.
